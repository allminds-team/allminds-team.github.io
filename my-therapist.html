Find a Therapist

Can I see a therapist in person?

All our therapists work exclusively online, because it allows you to see the best therapist for your needs, without geographical restrictions.

Are consultations really free?

Yes. We believe it's extremly important for you to meet your match and get a feel for their style of work. We won't even ask for your credit card information.

How much will my appointments cost?

Each therapist has different rates depending on their experience and specialization. We'll make sure to match you with someone that fits your budget.

Do you take my insurance?

Most therapists can work with your insurance out-of-network. We'll collect your insurance preferences, and find you the best match available.
