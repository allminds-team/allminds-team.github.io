Frequently asked questions

How much does Allminds pay?

We are proud to pay above market rates. Click "Apply now" above to view our current rates.

How do you get referrals?

We've partnered with major insurance companies to connect you with diverse patients in need of care. Don't worry, we take care of all the billling hassles, so you can just focus on your clients.

How fast can I grow my caseload?

On average, our clinicians get ~10 clients within a month after starting. We're purposefully limiting the size of our network so we can make sure we fill your caseload quickly.

Do you have minimum caseload requirements?

To help us expand access to mental health care, we expect you to maintain a caseload of at least 5 clients when possible.

Our mission

We envision a world where every human receives equitable mental health care, no matter their race, sexual orientation, gender, ability, language, citizenship, or more. This starts by empowering clinicians and unlocking their full potential.

I’m passionate about creating a space where other professionals of color can be heard, seen, feel valued, and celebrated for their expertise. In this way, I hope that more People of Color suffering from mental health issues will feel more comfortable seeking support and receiving the healing that is owed to them.

— Tangia Elieff, Allminds Practice Owner
