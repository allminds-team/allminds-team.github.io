Frequently asked questions

What is Allminds?

We’re a venture-backed mental health startup passionate about improving access to affordable, high-quality care. We make it easy for providers to accept insurance in their private practice, and connect them with clients looking for great, affordable care.

What does it cost to join?

Nothing! It’s completely free to join Allminds and get credentialed with all major insurance carriers. We make money in the long run by negotiating higher reimbursement fees with insurance companies, and taking a small cut of those extra earnings.

How much will I earn per session?

This varies by license type. To get more specific info, click "Get Started" right above and we’ll reach out to you within 24 hours.

What if I get referrals outside of Allminds?

Allminds is designed to support your entire practice. You can use our digital tools with all your clients whether they come to you through Allminds or not - and we will never take a cut from your cash-pay clients.

What's the commitment?

We don’t impose minimum hour commitments on providers in our community. Our mission is to make quality mental health care more accessible, and being able to help one client through our insurance program makes a differences.

Are there any requirements to join?

You need to be able to practice in California, have an individual NPI number, a fully completed, signed CAQH application (we can help with that) and a reliable internet connection.
