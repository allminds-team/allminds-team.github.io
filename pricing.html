How does the 30-day free trial work?

Simply create a new account to use all the features free for 30 days. Near the end of your trial, enter your billing information to continue your subscription without interruption.

Are there any upfront costs?

No - you can start enjoying all the benefits and features during your trial with no cost or obligation. No payment information is needed until you decide to continue.

Are there any other fees?

Nope. Our simple $49 plan includes every single feature we offer at this time.

Is Allminds HIPAA compliant?

Yes. All your account information is safely stored using the latest encryption standards. By signing up for our free 30 day trial, we mutually agree to our Business Associate Agreement.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Cancel your account at any time by navigating to Manage account > Payment and click on “Cancel my membership”.

What is your refund policy?

After your free 30-day trial, there are no contracts, and your subscription is paid on a month-to-month basis. You can cancel at any time, but you are responsible for any charges already incurred. We do not provide refunds.
