Building a world where everyone can be seen, heard and understood

Today's need

Mental health affects everyone, but culturally diverse communities - BIPOC/LGBTQIA - don't have equal access to care: medical racism, distrust in therapists, misdiagnoses, and lack of access to coverage. These are just a few of the barriers keeping diverse patients away from care.

We envision a world where every human receives equitable mental health care, no matter their race, sexual orientation, gender, ability, language, citizenship, or more. That's why we buit Allminds.

By 2045, 50% of all Americans will have at least one or more diverse identities, inclusing race, sexual orientation, gender identity and more.
