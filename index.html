Is Allminds HIPAA compliant?

Yes. All your account information is safely stored using the latest encryption standards. By signing up for our free 30 day trial, we instantly execute our Business Associate Agreement.

How does the 30-day free trial work?

Simply create a new account to use all the features free for 30 days - no credit card required. Near the end of your trial, choose a plan and enter your billing information to continue your subscription without interruption.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Cancel your account at any time and at no cost by navigating to Manage account > Payment and click on “Cancel my membership”.

Do you offer customer support?

You can get help via live chat, phone or email and speak directly with us, not a robot. We do our best to provide timely, thorough, and thoughtful responses within just a few hours - if not immediately :)
