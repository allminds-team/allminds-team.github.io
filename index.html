Frequently Asked Questions

What will I get out of therapy?

You'll get one-on one-support with a licensed therapist through weekly video meetings. You will learn proven techniques such as CBT and mindfulness to help you cope with life issues including depression, anxiety, trauma, and more.

Do you take insurance?

We are proud to accept a growing number of insurance plans. You can start booking an appointment to see if we accept yours. If we do, we will handle all the billing & paperwork!

Do I get to choose my own therapist?

Yes! You will choose from our available therapists based on your personal needs and preferences. At any point in your care, you may switch therapists if it's not a perfect fit.

How often can I see my therapist?

You will start with weekly sessions. Your provider will help you set the right therapeutic goals and will work with you until you have met them.
