Frequently asked questions

Can I see a therapist in person?

All of our therapists work exclusively online. This allows you to see the best therapist for your needs without geographical restrictions.

Are consultations really free?

Yes. We believe it's extremly important for you to meet your match and get a feel for their style of work. We won't even ask for your credit card information.

How much will my appointments cost?

Our therapists' rates range from $75 to $200 depending on their experience and specialization. Your out-of-pocket costs will depend on your insurance benefits, and we'll make sure to match you with someone that fits your budget.

Do you take my insurance?

If your health plan includes out-of-network benefits for mental health, we'll help you get reimbursed around 40-80% for each therapy session. At this time we do not offer in-network services.

How do I know if I have out-of-network benefits?

Call your insurance company directly and ask them if you have an out-of-network benefit for psychotherapy. If so, ask what percentage you will get reimbursed, and if you have a deductible.
