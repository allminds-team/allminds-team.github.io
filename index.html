Enhanced telehealth for therapists

Track your clients’ mental well-being and session ratings through app-less video and five-second surveys.

Early access is available by invitation only. Requests accepted until 09/31/2020.

Simple, HIPAA-compliant and app-less video

  • 1

    Send your client a link to launch the video

    The link can be opened from any mobile browser, tablet, or computer. No download required.

  • 2

    Jump into therapy right away

    Enjoy a secure, high quality and HIPAA-compliant video call with your client.

Frictionless and ultra-brief client surveys

  • 1

    Learn how your client is feeling before each appointment

    Your client will take five seconds to rate their well-being across different categories.

  • 2

    Get direct client feedback after each session

    Learn if they talked about what they wanted, if your approach was the right fit, and how they value your relationship.

  • 3

    Provide evidence-based mental health care

    Use this recurrent data to track how your clients are doing and see where they need the most support.
