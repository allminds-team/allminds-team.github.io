Frequently asked questions

What is Allminds?

Allminds is a brand new therapy service that surfaced from our own struggles trying to access affordable and culturally-competent mental health care.

What is the Early Access Program?

Our Early Access program is an opportunity for our first members to start therapy a discounted rate, as we make final tweaks to our platform.

Are there any requirements to join?

You must reside in California and be 18 years or older. In a few cases we may be legally or ethically unable to help, however we’ll still do everything we can to make sure you’re not left hanging.

How much does Allminds cost?

Our Early Access members will get therapy for $95 per session. We recommend you see your therapist once a week, but you are free to choose your own pace.
