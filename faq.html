FAQ

Search our FAQ for answers to anything you might ask.

General

What is Allminds? + −

Allminds is a telehealth platform specifically designed for therapists.

Key features include HIPAA compliant-video through browser (no download required), integrated client surveys to track well-being and session ratings, and a dashboard to keep actionable data at your fingertips.

How do I sign up? + −

We're limiting the number of Therapists that can use Allminds to ensure we can provide them with the best customer support. You can request an invitation here

and we will personally onboard you when your turn comes.
How much does Allminds cost? + −

Allminds is entirely free to use as we're committed to making Telehealth accessible. In the future we will release paid plans with extra features.

How much time does it take to get set up on Allminds? + −

You can be up and running in minutes. We’ve made it simple to get started with minimal required information for you to enter.

Security and Compliance

Is Allminds HIPAA compliant? + −

Yes, security of your account and electronic Patient Health Information (ePHI) is extremely important to us. We go above and beyond all the requirements of HIPAA as a business associate, including the Business Associate Agreement (BAA) in which Allminds agrees to be responsible for keeping all client information private.

Do you have a BAA? + −

When you create an account with Allminds, you also agree to our Terms of Service and Business Associate Agreement. Creating an account constitutes a legally binding agreement as stated when you click the Sign up button.

All features in our platform are covered in this agreement, including Telehealth.

There will be no need for you to agree to or "sign" the BAA again once you create an account. You can access a copy of the agreement here: Allminds BAA.

Are video sessions recorded and/or stored? + −

We do not record or store any video sessions.

Requirements to use Allminds

Do I need to install an app to use Allminds? + −

No. Allminds works entirely through your browser on any computer, laptop, tablet, or smartphone.

Will my clients have to install or download anything in order to join the session? + −

Nope. Your clients will be able to easily & effortlessly join the session by simply clicking a link you provide.

They will not have to:

  • Download or install software or extensions
  • Enter a password or ID to access the session
  • Create an account or login into an account
What browser versions are supported? + −

Allminds works on the latest versions of Google Chrome, Safari, Mozilla Firefox and Edge on desktops/laptops.

For Android, it can be run using Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox.

iOS devices can use Safari, Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox.

Note: You must ensure you and your clients are using the most up-to-date browser version to have the best experience. Please do not use Allminds on older or unsupported browser versions (like Internet Explorer) and be aware that incompatibilities can cause technical and video issues.

Features

How do client surveys work? + −

To help you take evidence-based decisions, we’ve designed an intuitive experience for clients to complete two ultra-brief surveys during each appointment.

Immediately upon clicking on the video link, clients are asked to visually rate their personal well-being, their close relationships, their social life and their overall well-being using a digital slider. Once they submit their answers, you will immediately be shown their ratings and you will be able to let your client into the video call.

After each session, clients will be asked to rate how they feel about their relationship with you, the topics of discussion, the approach you used, and their overall liking of the session.

We compute your clients’ scores in the background, and use that to create beautiful charts and tables that you can access through the dashboard to track how your clients are doing.

How long does it take clients to complete the surveys? + −

Each survey is designed to be completed in less than five seconds and be as effortless as possible.

Can I do group sessions? + −

We currently support group sessions with up to three participants, including yourself. This means you can see up to two other people at the same time. In the future, we will look to expand the number of participants allowed in group sessions even further.
